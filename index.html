ABOUT CITICAR

Car sharing makes personal vehicles affordable, accessible to all and safe for the city in the long term.

Thanks to cool technology, and forward-thinking entrepreneurs, we are redefining the way Kigali thinks about alternative transportation.

CITICAR is a transplant of the American concept ZIPCAR, which has also been duplicated in South Africa as LOCOMUTE. In Kigali, CITICAR will have designated locations around Kigali where the cars will be parked. Gaining access to car will be as simple as 4 steps.

  1. Clients will subscribe to the service prior to getting started.
  2. Once subscribed, the CITICAR member will book a car from one of our locations
  3. Pick up the vehicle
  4. Return the vehicle.

This transaction will be automated from start to finish, which makes it efficient. CITICAR is also an affordable solution for businesses and various organizations in Kigali.
